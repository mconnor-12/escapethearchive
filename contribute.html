The Living Archive

Contribute a Source
to the Archive

Archives are made, not found. Every document, photograph, recording, or account you submit becomes part of a growing collection, available to students and educators exploring memory, evidence, and the past.

What can I submit?

Photographs, documents, recordings, oral histories, objects, maps, or any source that captures a moment, past or present.

Who can contribute?

Students, educators, family members, community historians, researchers, or anyone with a source worth preserving.

What happens next?

Submissions are reviewed before entering the archive. You'll be credited according to your attribution preferences.

Source received.
